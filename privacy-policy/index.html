Privacy Policy

TeXtonic is a LaTeX editor and compiler. It runs entirely locally, on your iOS device. It does not require a network connection and therefore does not transmit your personal documents for processing.

TeXtonic does not collect any data, period.

The user can create files, which will reside in the application directory, and can be moved outside of the directory as needed by the user.

During the TestFlight phase, if the application crashes, a crash report can be sent to the developers. This crash report will contain information about what caused the crash (the contents of your personal documents).

The owner of the application, Brian Chevalier, does not have any way to access the data created by the user.
